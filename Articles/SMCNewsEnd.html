Farewell to SMC News: A Chapter Closes

Published on: 19/12/2024
Written

It's time to say goodbye to SMC News, our school newspaper. While it might not have been the most popular read, it did provide a platform for many students to express themselves and share their stories. Created by a team of students, the newspaper brought us updates on school events, sports highlights, and interesting articles about our peers and teachers.

The website, SMCNews.github.io, made by another student, will continue to host all the past articles, ensuring that the efforts of the SMC News team won't be forgotten. While the newspaper may not be missed by everyone, it's important to acknowledge the hard work and dedication that went into producing it.

So here's to SMC News and the students who contributed to it. Even if it wasn't always the most exciting publication, it still played a role in our school community. Goodbye, SMC News, and thank you for the memories.
