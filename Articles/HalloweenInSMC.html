Halloween Dressup Makes an Appearance In SMC!

Published on: 27/10/2023
Written by ??


On Wednesday the 18th of October, we took part in a dress up for Halloween. The atmosphere was electric throughout the school. Those who dressed up got the chance to take part in a competition for best dressed, exciting each student. To dress up every student donated two euros to Cairde Caher (Childcare in Louisburgh). Our photographer gathered and took some lovely photos of everyone on that day.
