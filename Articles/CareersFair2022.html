Careers Fair Event, a huge success!

Published on: 23/01/2023
Written by Martha Weston


2022-23 Leaving Certs enjoying the FairFrom left Charlie Morrison, ATU Professor, Cian O’Malley and Ciaran Jennings

On Thursday the 24th of November, a Careers fair was held in Sancta Maria College. It was a great opportunity for students and parents to see what options are out there for students once they finish secondary school.

This was an event in which many different colleges, courses and people from lots of different careers attended to give information to students, a place where students were able to ask questions and talk to people with advice on where they should go after finishing school.

It included information on colleges such as NUIG and Hibernia college, people from different career paths such as teaching, medicine, law and many more and other options such as apprenticeships.

The volunteers were open to questions and very knowledgeable, what a great opportunity for everyone involved.

Many Students and Parents attended the Fair, it was an enjoyable experience for all who attended.

Thank’s to Ms Gill who organised the whole event. It was a great success! And we look forward to more events like this in the future.
